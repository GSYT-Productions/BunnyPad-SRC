BunnyPad Theme Usage Notice

BunnyPad now supports per-user customization. Users can edit their theme by modifying:
   %USERPROFILE%\.bunnypad\qss\stylesheet.qss

To reset to the default theme, delete or rename the ".bunnypad" folder in your user directory.
